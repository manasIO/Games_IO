## About the Game

Mouse Shooter is a minimalist survival shooter built entirely with HTML5 Canvas and vanilla JavaScript. You control a turret at the center of the arena and must fend off waves of enemies that swarm in from all four edges of the screen. The longer you survive and the more enemies you eliminate, the higher your score — and the harder it gets.

---

## How to Play

| Action | Control |
|---|---|
| Aim | Move your mouse |
| Shoot | Left click |
| Restart (after game over) | Left click |

---

## Features

- **Wave-based difficulty** — enemies get faster and tougher as your score climbs
- **Two enemy types** — standard red enemies and elite purple enemies with more HP
- **Health bars** — shown on multi-HP enemies so you know how many shots they need
- **Explosion particles** — visual feedback on every hit and kill
- **Crosshair** — custom cursor that replaces the default pointer
- **HUD** — live score, current wave, and 5-heart life counter
- **Hit feedback** — your turret flashes red when an enemy reaches you
- **Game over screen** — shows your final score and wave reached; click to restart
